Eisenhower matrix

One effective way to defeat procrastination or laziness is by using the Eisenhower Matrix, a simple yet powerful framework that sorts tasks according to urgency and importance. This approach helps you focus on high-priority tasks first, delegate or schedule less urgent items, and eliminate time-wasting activities. By integrating the Eisenhower Matrix into your daily routine, you’ll experience immediate improvements in time management, reduce feeling overwhelmed, and start making steady progress toward your goals.
